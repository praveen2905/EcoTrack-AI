# EcoTrackAI 🌱

## Overview

EcoTrackAI is an AI-powered web platform designed to help individuals understand, track, and reduce their carbon footprint through simple daily actions and personalized recommendations. The platform promotes sustainable living by analyzing user activities and providing actionable insights to lower environmental impact.

---

## Chosen Vertical

**Sustainability & Climate Action**

EcoTrackAI focuses on environmental sustainability by addressing the growing need for individuals to monitor their carbon emissions and make eco-friendly lifestyle choices.

---

## Approach and Logic

The solution follows a user-centric approach:

1. **Data Collection**

   * Users provide information about their daily activities, such as transportation, electricity consumption, food habits, and waste generation.

2. **Carbon Footprint Calculation**

   * The system uses predefined emission factors to estimate carbon emissions generated by different activities.

3. **AI-Powered Analysis**

   * AI analyzes user behavior patterns and identifies the major sources of carbon emissions.

4. **Personalized Recommendations**

   * Based on the analysis, the platform suggests practical actions to reduce environmental impact, such as using public transport, reducing energy consumption, or adopting sustainable dietary habits.

5. **Progress Tracking**

   * Users can monitor their carbon footprint over time through visual dashboards and performance metrics.

---

## How the Solution Works

### Step 1: User Input

Users enter information related to:

* Transportation habits
* Energy consumption
* Food preferences
* Waste management practices

### Step 2: Emission Estimation

The platform calculates estimated CO₂ emissions for each category using standard carbon emission conversion factors.

### Step 3: AI Insights Generation

AI processes the collected data to:

* Identify high-emission activities
* Compare user behavior with sustainable benchmarks
* Generate personalized recommendations

### Step 4: Visualization Dashboard

The results are displayed through:

* Carbon footprint score
* Emission category breakdown
* Progress trends
* Sustainability achievements

### Step 5: Action & Improvement

Users receive customized action plans and can track reductions in their carbon footprint over time.

---

## Assumptions Made

* Users provide accurate information regarding their daily activities.
* Standard carbon emission factors are used for estimation purposes.
* The calculated footprint represents an approximation and may not reflect exact real-world emissions.
* Internet connectivity is available for accessing AI-based recommendations and analytics.
* Recommendations are generalized and intended for awareness and behavioral improvement rather than professional environmental assessments.

---

## Conclusion

EcoTrackAI empowers individuals to make informed decisions about their environmental impact. By combining carbon footprint tracking, AI-driven insights, and personalized recommendations, the platform encourages sustainable habits and contributes toward a greener future.
