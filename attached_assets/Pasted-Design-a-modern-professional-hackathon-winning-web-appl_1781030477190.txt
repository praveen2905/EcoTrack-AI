Design a modern, professional, hackathon-winning web application called EcoTrack AI that helps users understand, track, and reduce their carbon footprint through AI-powered insights and gamification.

Design Style

- Modern SaaS dashboard design
- Clean, premium, and futuristic UI
- Green and blue eco-friendly color palette
- Smooth animations and micro-interactions
- Fully responsive (desktop, tablet, mobile)
- Glassmorphism and modern card layouts
- Professional typography
- Dark Mode and Light Mode support
